Teriyaki Chicken

Succulent chicken breast glazed with a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, served with vegetables.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook chicken until browned and cooked through.
  4. Pour the teriyaki sauce into the pan and simmer for 3–4 minutes.
  5. Mix cornstarch with water to create a slurry.
  6. Add the slurry to the sauce and cook until thickened.
  7. Coat the chicken thoroughly in the sauce.
  8. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
  9. Serve with steamed rice.
