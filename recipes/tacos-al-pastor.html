Tacos al Pastor

Marinated pork cooked on a vertical spit, served in soft tortillas with pineapple and cilantro.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Remove stems and seeds from guajillo and ancho chiles, then soak them in hot water for 10 minutes.
  2. Blend soaked chiles, garlic, pineapple juice, vinegar, oregano, cumin, paprika, pepper, and salt until smooth.
  3. Marinate the sliced pork in the sauce for at least 2 hours
  4. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and cook the pork until browned and slightly crispy.
  5. Warm the corn tortillas.
  6. Fill tortillas with pork, diced pineapple, onion, and cilantro.
  7. Serve with lime wedges.
