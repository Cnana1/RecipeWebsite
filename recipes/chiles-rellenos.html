Chiles Rellenos

Roasted poblano peppers stuffed with cheese and covered in a light egg batter and sauce.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Roast the poblano peppers over an open flame or in the oven until the skin blisters.
  2. Place them in a bowl and cover for 10 minutes, then peel the skin off.
  3. Cut a small slit in each pepper and remove the seeds.
  4. Stuff the peppers with shredded cheese.
  5. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, then fold in the egg yolks.
  6. Lightly coat each stuffed pepper in flour.
  7. Dip peppers in the egg batter.
  8. Heat oil in a skillet and fry peppers until golden brown.
  9. In another pan, sauté onion and garlic, then add tomato sauce and simmer 5 minutes.
  10. Serve the fried peppers with warm tomato sauce.
