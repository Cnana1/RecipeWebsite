Pad Thai

Thai stir-fried noodles with shrimp, peanuts, and tamarind sauce, topped with a squeeze of lime.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 20 minutes until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook shrimp or chicken until done, then set aside.
  4. Add garlic to the pan and sauté briefly.
  5. Push ingredients to one side and scramble the egg.
  6. Add softened noodles to the pan.
  7. Stir in fish sauce, tamarind paste, brown sugar, and lime juice.
  8. Add bean sprouts, carrots, green onions, and cooked protein.
  9. Toss everything together for 2–3 minutes.
  10. Garnish with crushed peanuts and serve with lime wedges.
