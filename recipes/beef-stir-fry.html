Beef Stir Fry

Tender beef and fresh vegetables quickly cooked in a savory soy-based sauce over rice.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Toss sliced beef with soy sauce and cornstarch and let marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Cook beef quickly until browned, then remove from pan.
  4. Add garlic and vegetables to the pan and stir fry for 3–4 minutes.
  5. Return beef to the pan.
  6. Stir in oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil.
  7. Toss everything together until well coated.
  8. Serve hot over cooked rice.
